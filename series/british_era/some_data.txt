Title: British Era Series - Production Data

Production Year: 2022
Genre: Historical Drama
Director: Ama Yaa Boateng
Producer: Kwabena Agyei
Screenplay: Akosua Mensah
Cinematography: Kofi Asante
Music: Nana Yaa Boateng

Production Budget: GHS 70 million
Filming Locations:
- Cape Coast Castle
- Accra, Ghana
- Aburi Botanical Gardens

Release Date: March 12, 2022
Runtime: 130 minutes
Language: Twi and English

Awards and Nominations:
- Best Director (Ama Yaa Boateng) - Winner
- Best Actor (Kwame Agyei) - Nominated
- Best Actress (Abena Mensah) - Nominated
- Best Original Score (Nana Yaa Boateng) - Winner

Box Office: GHS 120 million (worldwide)

Trivia:
- The film's historical accuracy was praised by historians and scholars.
- Many scenes were shot on location at Cape Coast Castle, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
- "British Era Series" aims to educate and entertain, shedding light on Ghana's colonial history.
