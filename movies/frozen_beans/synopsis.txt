Title: Frozen_Beans - Synopsis

In the picturesque Ghanaian village of Akoroma, a sudden cold spell devastates the crops, threatening the livelihood of the community. Anna, a resourceful young woman, embarks on a journey to save her village from hunger.

She teams up with John, a passionate farmer, to find a solution. As their quest unfolds, they stumble upon ancient secrets, magical beans, and a chilling mystery that could change their lives forever.

Frozen_Beans is a heartwarming tale of courage, friendship, and the power of unity as the villagers work together to overcome the unexpected frozen curse.
