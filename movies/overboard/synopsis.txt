Title: Overboard - Synopsis

Kwame, a humble fisherman in the coastal town of Axim, Ghana, faces a life-changing twist when he rescues Ama, a wealthy and spoiled heiress, after her yacht capsizes. Due to a case of amnesia, Ama is unable to remember her privileged life.

Kwame seizes the opportunity to teach Ama the value of hard work and the beauty of simplicity, convincing her that they are married and struggling to make ends meet. As Ama adapts to her new life, she discovers the true meaning of love, community, and the treasures of the Ghanaian coast.

Overboard is a heartwarming romantic comedy set against the backdrop of Ghana's stunning beaches and vibrant culture.
