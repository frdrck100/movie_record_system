Title: Dark_Moon - Synopsis

In the bustling city of Accra, a series of gruesome murders shock the community. Detective Mensah, a seasoned investigator, is called in to solve the case. As he delves deeper into the twisted mind of the killer, he uncovers a web of secrets that threaten to consume him.

Meanwhile, Emily, a young woman with a haunting past, finds herself at the center of the investigation. Her dreams are plagued by visions of the murders, and she becomes an unlikely ally to Detective Mensah.

As the body count rises, the two must unravel the enigma behind the Dark_Moon killer before it's too late. In a world where nothing is as it seems, trust is a luxury they can't afford.

Dark_Moon is a gripping thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the final, shocking revelation.
