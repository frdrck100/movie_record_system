Title: Silent Whalers - Synopsis

In the fishing village of Axim, Ghana, a close-knit community depends on the sea for their livelihood. When the village faces the threat of overfishing and dwindling catches, Kwame, a young fisherman, embarks on a quest to find solutions.

As Kwame's journey unfolds, he uncovers a hidden world of illegal whaling operations threatening the marine ecosystem. With the help of Abena, an environmental activist, and a group of determined villagers, they embark on a perilous mission to expose the culprits and save their way of life.

Silent Whalers is a powerful drama highlighting the importance of environmental conservation and the resilience of coastal communities in Ghana.
